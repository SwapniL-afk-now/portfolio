About Me

I'm Ismam Nur Swapnil, an Electrical and Electronic Engineering graduate from BUET, specializing in Large Language Models (LLMs), Vision-Language Models (VLMs), Reinforcement Learning (RL), Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT), and AI Agents. My work includes developing a VLM-based chatbot for skin disease detection with RAG and RLHF, an LLM-powered research paper analysis tool using LLaMA-3.1, and optimizing models through SFT and model pruning.

Skilled in Python, PyTorch, and TensorFlow, I’m passionate about advancing multimodal AI and agent-based systems. Honored with BUET’s Merit Scholarship and Dean’s List, I’m driven to innovate in AI.
